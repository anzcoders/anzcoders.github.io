FAQ

Where is it?
It's virtual. It's wherever you are at the time. No need to worry about transport or parking for this conference.

What's the cost?
It's free! Zero. Nada. Nothing. Squat.
Why? Because we love you. Awww. #hugs

Will dinner be provided?
No, but feel free to buy yourself a pizza and drop bits all over the floor at home if you want that full conference experience.

When is registration open
Now! Go register!

How does voting work?
Once sessions close we'll be putting all the abstracts up on UserVoice. The community will then have a few weeks to decide how to spend their votes. The top voted submissions will be asked to present and we'll work out the schedule for you after that.

I want to speak!
Wonderful! We want you to as well. Submit a session and if the commnunity votes for it, you're on!

I want to help!
